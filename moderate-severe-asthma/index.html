Asthma is an inflammatory disease with recurring flare-ups of inflammation and symptoms.4

Moderate/ Severe Asthma

Asthma attacks continue to impact patients irrespective of their disease severity, asthma control or adherence to current asthma medications.5

Patients and HCPs continue to rely on SABA relievers, which do not treat the inflammatory flare-up, leaving patients at risk of preventable attacks, whether adherent or not to a maintenance controller.6
