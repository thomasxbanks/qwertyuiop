ReframingRespIratory

AstraZeneca is committed to Reframing Respiratory Care by supporting three fundamental changes needed to advance clinical practice:

  1. Reducing the reliance on rescue inhalers and establishing anti-inflammatory reliever as the foundation of asthma care
  2. Eliminating the dependency on chronic oral corticosteroids and improving severe asthma care
  3. Enabling earlier biology-driven treatment of respiratory disease by defining the eosinophilic phenotype
