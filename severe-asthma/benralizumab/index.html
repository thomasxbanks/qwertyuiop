Compound:

Benralizumab

Benralizumab is the only humanised, anti-IL-5 receptor monoclonal antibody that induces active eosinophil apoptosis via antibody-dependent cell-mediated cytoxicity.2

Benralizumab reduced exacerbations, improved lung function and reduced OCS dose in patients with severe uncontrolled asthma.2
